How it works

A simple way to discover learning resources

KimyGuide was built for people who know what they want to learn but are not sure where to start. Instead of browsing endlessly, you can describe your goal and receive focused recommendations.

1

Enter your goal

Type what you want to learn in natural language, such as learning a language, improving technical skills, or exploring a new topic.

2

Explore recommendations

The system compares your goal with course content and returns a ranked list of educational resources that best match your request.

3

Find a starting point

Use the results as a practical starting point for your learning journey, especially when you are new to a subject and need guidance.

Why KimyGuide

Designed for people who need direction at the start

Many learners arrive on educational platforms with a clear goal but no prior history. KimyGuide was designed to support that moment by helping users discover relevant courses from the very beginning.

The system was developed as a final-year Computer Science project exploring goal-driven recommendation for educational content under cold-start conditions.

Goal-driven

Recommendations begin with what the learner wants to achieve, not with past clicks or browsing history.

Learner-friendly

The interface is designed to be simple and approachable, especially for first-time users exploring new subjects.

Built on real course data

The system uses a curated dataset of educational content, helping make the recommendations more realistic and practical.
